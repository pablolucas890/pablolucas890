## üöÄ About Me

I'm a **Full Stack Developer** at [AmoraDev](https://www.amoradev.com.br/), working with **IoT** and applications for [Controllar](https://controllar.com/) ‚Äî a home automation company. We support **Debian** on SBC boards (Raspberry Pi, Orange Pi), integrating hardware via **MQTT** to send and receive RF/IR commands through blasters and our own signal boards, all managed with **Python**.

- **APIs** in **Node.js** on the boards, using **Docker** to communicate with **React Native** apps for automation commands
- **VPN** for secure remote access so users can automate from anywhere
- Custom **touch panels** with open-source firmware like [Tasmota](https://tasmota.github.io/), plus APIs and config apps

We also maintain **OS images** for SBCs with a system built in **Vue** and **Node.js**. Using [DEBOS](https://github.com/go-debos/debos) and Debian base images, technicians can add `.ovpn` files and client-specific databases and generate bootable images with one click ‚Äî with our systems, services, and Docker images pre-configured.

Beyond automation, we work on **IoT** for beer tap temperature control and refrigerator on/off switching using **ESP** microcontrollers, with apps in **React Native**.

I also support **technical operations** at [IPPO](https://ippo.com.br/) ‚Äî a startup that runs weekly check-ins for nutritionists‚Äô patients, using **AI** to collect health data and improve professional decisions. Everything is integrated with **WhatsApp**: the AI collects, analyzes, and returns personalized feedback for patients and professionals, with data on a dashboard for follow-up, prompts, history, and risk analysis.

We build **free apps** for initiatives aligned with our purpose: *"Empowering people by developing impactful ideas."* Examples: the **Full Moon Festival** (annual solid waste collection and prizes) and **Mundo Recicladores** ‚Äî a system where collectors and recyclers sell invoices to large companies and receive a share of the value.

During my degree, I led a project on **data collection** from chemical and physical experiments in electron energy loss spectroscopy, using much cheaper plates and components than market alternatives, helping universities with limited budgets. I‚Äôve also registered a **data management system for university infirmaries** with the INPI and worked with **Virtual Reality**, developing plugins for **Meta Quest** headsets.
